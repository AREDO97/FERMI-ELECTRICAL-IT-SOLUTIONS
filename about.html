Who We Are

Fermi Electrical & IT Solutions is a multidisciplinary team delivering end-to-end electrical, IT, and security system installations—including fire alarms, access control, and surveillance—engineered for reliability, compliance, and seamless operational integration.

  • Installations performed in line with IEC standards and local regulations.
  • Connectivity Solutions: Fast, secure WAN/LAN, plus structured cabling.
  • Backup power: UPS, solar-hybrid, generator integration.
  • Commercial/industrial wiring, panels, elevators & escalators interfaces.
  • Access control, CCTV, fire, motion detection & Alarm system installations.
